Quick Start Guide

Set up in 30 seconds.
Start auditing now.

Four steps to become a certified civic auditor — no account needed to begin.

01
👁
Step 01
See a Problem

Spot a garbage pile, blocked footpath, traffic snarl or dirty public space. No registration required to begin auditing your city.

02
📷
Step 02
Capture Evidence

Take a photo or record a short video. GPS auto-fills. Add your 6-digit pincode and a landmark. Video evidence earns 25 bonus auditor points.

03
📋
Step 03
Submit Your Audit

Select category, set severity level, add your description. Your audit is logged on the public record immediately and appears on the live map.

04
🏛
Step 04
Earn Your Badge

Track your audit from Open to Resolved in real time. Each audit earns points toward your Citizen Auditor Badge — from Observer to Guardian level.

You are ready
Open the map. View incidents. Filter by type or radius.
Select your Alert Zone and get notified of incidents near your home or workplace.
